**Everything You Need to Know — From Hospital Discharge Through the First Month**

---

## "If Only I'd Had a List, I Would Have Worried Less..."

On the car ride home from the hospital, many first-time parents find themselves thinking:

"It's just the two of us now, and we're responsible for this tiny life..."

At the hospital, nurses were always nearby. Help was just a button press away. But now, at home, you have to do everything on your own.

What do you need to prepare? What do you need to check? What's normal, and what's a problem?

This checklist answers all of those questions. From pre-birth preparations to daily care and emergencies — everything first-time parents need to know, organized step by step.

Print it out and stick it on the refrigerator. Pull it out whenever you feel anxious. You are doing better than you think.

## 3. Daily Care Checklist

### Morning Routine

<div class="bg-white dark:bg-slate-800 rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-slate-100 dark:border-slate-700 overflow-hidden my-3">
  <div class="bg-orange-500 px-5 py-3">
    <h4 class="font-bold text-white text-sm mb-0">⏰ Morning Routine</h4>
  </div>
  <div class="p-5 grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-x-8">
    <div>
      <p class="text-xs font-bold text-slate-500 mb-2">When Baby Wakes</p>
      <ul class="text-sm space-y-1 mb-0">
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Check / change diaper</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Prepare for feeding</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Gently wipe baby's face</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Ventilate the room</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Check room temperature</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div>
      <p class="text-xs font-bold text-slate-500 mb-2">Feeding (Every 2–3 Hours)</p>
      <ul class="text-sm space-y-1 mb-0">
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Wash hands thoroughly</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Find a comfortable position</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Note the start time of feeding</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> 10–15 minutes per breast (breastfeeding)</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Burp the baby</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Record amount and time</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Calculate the next feeding time</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="bg-white dark:bg-slate-800 rounded-lg shadow-sm p-5 border-l-4 border-orange-400 my-4">
  <h4 class="font-bold text-orange-700 dark:text-orange-400 mb-3">🧷 Diapering (8–12 Times Per Day)</h4>
  <div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-x-6">
    <ul class="text-sm space-y-1 mb-0">
      <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Wash hands</li>
      <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Have all supplies within reach</li>
      <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Never leave baby unattended</li>
      <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Wipe front to back (for girls)</li>
    </ul>
    <ul class="text-sm space-y-1 mb-0">
      <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Dry completely</li>
      <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Apply rash cream (if needed)</li>
      <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Put on a fresh diaper</li>
      <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Wash hands again</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

## 12. Frequently Asked Questions Checklist

<div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-4 my-4">
  <div class="bg-white dark:bg-slate-800 rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-slate-100 dark:border-slate-700 overflow-hidden">
    <div class="bg-orange-500 px-5 py-3">
      <h4 class="font-bold text-white text-sm mb-0">🍼 Feeding</h4>
    </div>
    <div class="p-5">
      <ul class="text-sm space-y-1 mb-0">
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> How often? → Every 2–3 hours, 8–12 times per day</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> How much at a time? → Follow baby's hunger cues</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Is baby eating enough? → At least 6 wet diapers per day</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> What if baby doesn't burp? → Try for 10 minutes, then it's okay to stop</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="bg-white dark:bg-slate-800 rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-slate-100 dark:border-slate-700 overflow-hidden">
    <div class="bg-teal-500 px-5 py-3">
      <h4 class="font-bold text-white text-sm mb-0">😴 Sleep</h4>
    </div>
    <div class="p-5">
      <ul class="text-sm space-y-1 mb-0">
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> How many hours per day? → 14–17 hours</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Day/night confusion? → Keep it bright during the day, dark at night</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Can we co-sleep? → Not recommended for safety reasons</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="bg-white dark:bg-slate-800 rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-slate-100 dark:border-slate-700 overflow-hidden">
    <div class="bg-blue-500 px-5 py-3">
      <h4 class="font-bold text-white text-sm mb-0">🛁 Bathing</h4>
    </div>
    <div class="p-5">
      <ul class="text-sm space-y-1 mb-0">
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Every day? → Daily or every other day</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Before the umbilical cord falls off? → Sponge bath only</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Water temperature? → 98–100°F (37–38°C)</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="bg-white dark:bg-slate-800 rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-slate-100 dark:border-slate-700 overflow-hidden">
    <div class="bg-purple-500 px-5 py-3">
      <h4 class="font-bold text-white text-sm mb-0">💩 Bowel Movements</h4>
    </div>
    <div class="p-5">
      <ul class="text-sm space-y-1 mb-0">
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> How many times per day? → Breastfed: 3–4+, Formula: 1–2</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> What color? → Yellow and green are both normal</li>
        <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Constipation? → Rare in newborns; consult a doctor</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="bg-white dark:bg-slate-800 rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-slate-100 dark:border-slate-700 overflow-hidden my-4">
  <div class="bg-amber-500 px-5 py-3">
    <h4 class="font-bold text-white text-sm mb-0">😢 Crying</h4>
  </div>
  <div class="p-5">
    <ul class="text-sm space-y-1 mb-0">
      <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> How many hours per day? → 1–3 hours is normal</li>
      <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> What if baby won't stop? → Check for hunger, dirty diaper, tiredness, or discomfort</li>
      <li class="flex gap-2"><span class="text-slate-400">☐</span> Colic? → Worse in the evenings; typically resolves by 3 months</li>
    </ul>
