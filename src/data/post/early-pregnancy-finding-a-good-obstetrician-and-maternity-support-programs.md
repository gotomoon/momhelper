In the US, you can easily find reputable doctors and hospitals through websites like Healthgrades and Zocdoc.

- **Healthgrades**: [https://www.healthgrades.com](https://www.healthgrades.com)
- **Zocdoc**: [https://www.zocdoc.com](https://www.zocdoc.com)

## How to Apply for Insurance and Maternity Support Programs

In the United States, in most cases, costs are covered through existing insurance. However, for mothers who need additional support for various reasons, there are several programs available.

### Health Insurance Marketplace

On the Marketplace, you can compare and choose from various insurance plans related to pregnancy and childbirth, and you may receive subsidies based on your income.

**How to Use:**
1. Visit [Healthcare.gov](https://www.healthcare.gov)
2. Create an account and enter your personal information
3. Compare and select an insurance plan
4. Enter your income information to determine your eligibility for subsidies

### WIC (Women, Infants, and Children)

WIC is a nutrition support program for low-income pregnant women and children.

**How to Apply:**
1. Find a local WIC office on the [WIC website](https://www.fns.usda.gov/wic)
2. Fill out the application and schedule an appointment
3. Submit proof of income, identification, proof of residence, and other required documents

### Medicaid

Medicaid assists with pregnancy and childbirth costs for low-income families.

**How to Apply:**
1. Visit [Medicaid.gov](https://www.medicaid.gov)
2. Fill out the application through your state's Medicaid office
3. Submit documents such as proof of income, identification, and proof of residence

### CHIP (Children's Health Insurance Program)

CHIP provides health insurance for children and can also benefit pregnant women.

**How to Apply:**
1. Visit [InsureKidsNow.gov](https://www.insurekidsnow.gov)
2. Fill out the application through your state's CHIP program
3. Submit the necessary documents to verify your eligibility

### Local Health Department

Local health departments offer various pregnancy support programs. You can find information about local health departments on each state's health department website. Visit your local health department's website or search "Local health department near me" on Google to check available programs.

### Nonprofit Organizations

There are nonprofit organizations that run pregnancy and childbirth support programs:

- **Planned Parenthood** - They offer various support services related to pregnancy and childbirth
- **March of Dimes** - They run programs for the health of pregnant women and newborns
- **Google Search** - You can find nearby nonprofit organizations by searching with keywords like "pregnancy support nonprofit near me"
