## 1. Why You Need a Postpartum Care Helper

### Korea vs. U.S. Postpartum Reality

<div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-4 my-6">
  <div class="bg-blue-50 border border-blue-200 rounded-lg p-5">
    <h4 class="font-bold text-blue-700 mb-3">Korean Postpartum Norm</h4>
    <ul class="text-sm space-y-1 mb-0">
      <li>At least 3 weeks focused recovery</li>
      <li>Family or sanhujoriwon support</li>
      <li>Warm, nourishing postpartum meals</li>
      <li>Recovery is culturally expected</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="bg-orange-50 border border-orange-200 rounded-lg p-5">
    <h4 class="font-bold text-orange-700 mb-3">U.S. Postpartum Reality</h4>

      <li>Hospital discharge often in 1-2 days</li>
      <li>Limited family support nearby</li>
      <li>Traditional postpartum care is less common</li>
      <li>Recovery + newborn care happen at once</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
